What RUN-A-LOAN
can do for you

Enhances your image

Professional documents and communications ensuring your corporate branding is consistent. Our customer portal and WhatsApp channels provide online application channels for your business. The advanced functionality of the RUN-A-LOAN system will make you to stand out from crowd.

Expands your products

Nano loans, payday loans, micro-loans, home loans, vehicle loans, taxi loans, building loans, agricultural loans, savings products, reward accounts and more. If you can lend it, RUN-A-LOAN can manage it.

Ensures compliance

Not only in terms of users complying with your business rules but regulatory compliance too. Audit-ability of all financial transactions in the system is standard.

Increases profitability

Reducing the administrative burden, increasing your collections rate and improving turnaround times. This together with automation and the flexibility to adjust your offerings to meet market demands, quickly and efficiently, will certainly increase your profitability.

Saves you money

You will not need any additional IT infrastructure or staff. No need to worry about disaster recovery, backups, monitoring etc as this is all taken care of. You can focus on taking care of your business.

Saves you time

Streamlined processes, automated collections, disbursements, scoring, approvals and comprehensive reporting capabilities. Real-time accounting package integration is also available, removing the need for manual interventions.
